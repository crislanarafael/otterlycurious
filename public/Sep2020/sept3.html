After a fair bit of time planning as well as going back and forth on design and layout choices and web hosting I'm proud to launch my personal blog this September!

There are so many choices for blogging platforms such as Wordpress being the most popular platform but I've decided to create the whole thing using pure HTML and CSS only. I'll be making sure to add some javascript that I've picked up recently wherever I see fit.

Why HTML/CSS you say? I have a few reasons. A big one is to help keep me practicing coding everyday, another one is honestly to have complete freedom on how I want to design my page, and what mistakes/time I'll need to take to get there. Just yesterday I was formatting a personalized divider (with an otter on it obviously) and it took me some time to figure it out thanks to stackoverflow!

On here you can find my personal projects, progress, things I find interesting that I want to archive for later and share with you, and of course thoughts. As a student, aspiring software engineer, and pilot, I'm dedicating this place towards tracking my progress in what I want to achieve. Thanks for reading!
